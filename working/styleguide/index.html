Principles

Plain, useful, and calm.

Readable first

Use generous spacing, high contrast, and type sizes that survive real content.

Clear hierarchy

Make primary actions obvious, secondary actions quiet, and labels unambiguous.

Reusable pieces

Prefer small components with predictable variants over one-off page styling.
